Yoga In The Park!

On June 19, we enjoyed a beautiful, sunny day at our annual Yoga in the Park event! Our amazing staff and members of the local community came together for a wonderful morning of yoga under the sun. We also shared delicious local treats from the Italian bakery Mario’s and refreshing lemonade from Del’s.

If you are interested in hosting a community event with us, please feel free to reach out to us!
